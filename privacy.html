Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Hosting

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc., 88 Colin P Kelly Jr St, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA). When you visit, GitHub's servers automatically log standard access data including your IP address, browser type, and the requested URL. This data is processed by GitHub for technical security purposes under their Privacy Statement. We have no access to these server logs.

No Cookies & Tracking

This website sets no cookies and uses no analytics software, advertising networks, or tracking pixels of any kind. Your language preference is detected once using the browser's built-in navigator.language API — this value is never stored or transmitted anywhere.

App Data Storage

The BoxLabel app stores all data you create — box names, contents, and item photos — exclusively on your device. Nothing is transmitted to external servers. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or share it yourself.

Camera Access

The app requests camera permission to scan QR code labels and to add photos of box contents. All scanning and photo capture happens entirely on-device. No images or video frames are uploaded or processed remotely at any point.

Universal Links

Box QR codes encode a URL of the form /box/UUID. Following such a link triggers a client-side JavaScript redirect that opens the corresponding box in the BoxLabel app. No personal data is recorded server-side when this link is followed. The UUID is used solely as an identifier within the deep link.
